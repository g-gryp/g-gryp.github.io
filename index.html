Projects

During the first year of my PhD at Imperial College, I designed a versatile, high-precision, low power instrument for neural recordings during Deep Brain Stimulation. The device was tested and shown to fit the required specifications. An updated modular approach was also proposed and will be manufactured during the second year of my PhD. As part of the recording device, a novel modification was proposed and verified to the standard Bainter notch filter topology which allows the filter to be one-resistor tunable.
